Letterfitting & type design tools inspired by computational neuroscience

Can we objectively measure
beauty and legibility?

The next generation of type design tools will be powered by neuroscience and machine learning. They will understand what your designs look like to humans, and help you make optimizations accordingly. But how will that work?

YinYangFit is an independent research & development project, funded in part by Google.

This is a work in progress and your contributions are welcomed! Get in touch via Github or contact me via email.
